## New Task (Habit Formation)

1. **Define the Habit Clearly**
   - Specify the habit (e.g., "Read 10 pages daily").
   - Associate it with a purpose or goal (e.g., "Improve knowledge to excel at work").

2. **Break It into Micro Tasks**
   - Divide the habit into small steps:
      - Example: Day 1: Read 2 pages. Day 2: Increase to 5 pages.
   - Ensure tasks require minimal effort to start.

3. **Set a Fixed Time and Place**
   - Link the habit to a specific time or trigger:
      - Example: "Stretch after waking up."
   - Use existing routines to anchor the habit.

4. **Track Consistency**
   - Provide a visual tracker:
      - Calendar with streak highlights.
      - Progress bars for milestones like 3, 7, or 21 days.
   - Allow users to log reflections (e.g., "How did it feel today?").

5. **Introduce a Reward System**
   - **Immediate Rewards**:
      - Motivational quotes or celebratory animations.
   - **Milestone Rewards**:
      - Day 3: "Mini-Master Badge."
      - Day 7: Unlock a new app feature.
   - **Custom Rewards**:
      - Let users set personal rewards (e.g., "Buy a treat after 10-day streak").

6. **Gamify the Process**
   - Add levels and badges:
      - Example: Level 1: Beginner, Level 5: Habit Pro.
   - Introduce streak bonuses:
      - Example: "Complete 7 days to unlock guided relaxation."
   - Optional leaderboard for friendly competition.

7. **Overcome Barriers**
   - Identify obstacles (e.g., time, forgetfulness).
   - Provide solutions:
      - Nudges and reminders.
      - Suggest micro-tasks on busy days.

8. **Reflect and Adjust**
   - Weekly reflection:
      - “What worked well? What was challenging?”
   - Suggest adjustments:
      - Reduce task complexity or change the timing.

9. **Celebrate Success**
   - Daily: Share affirmations after completion.
   - Weekly: Visualize progress with streak counters.
   - Monthly: Offer certificates or highlight achievements.

10. **Transition to Autopilot**
    - As the habit stabilizes:
       - Reduce reminders.
       - Encourage intrinsic motivation.
       - Suggest stacking new habits.
